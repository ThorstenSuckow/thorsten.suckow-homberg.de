BSI IT-Grundschutz Compendium

IT-Grundschutz Compendium​

In addition to the standards of the BSI IT-Grundschutz, the BSI IT-Grundschutz Compendium1 provides an extensive catalog of threats, their potential impact, and recommendations for safeguards. It includes ready-to-use templates and building blocks that help organizations implement tailored protective measures across various IT scenarios.
