## IT-Grundschutz Compendium

In addition to the standards of the <Wiki name="BSI IT-Grundschutz" url="csec.bsigrundschutz" />, the **BSI IT-Grundschutz Compendium**[^sources] provides an extensive catalog of threats, their potential impact, and recommendations for safeguards.
It includes ready-to-use templates and building blocks that help organizations implement tailored protective measures across various IT scenarios.
